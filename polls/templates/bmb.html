Bhag Milkha Bhag

Director: Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra

Writer: Prasoon Joshi

Genre: Action, Sports, Biography, History

Stars: Farhan Akhtar, Sonam Kapoor, Pavan Malhotra

The PLOT

The story of runner Milkha Singh (Farhan Akhtar), called "the Flying Sikh," shows his loss in the 400-meter finals at the 1960 Olympics. The truth behind the ascension of Milkha "The Flying Sikh" Singh who was scarred because of the India-Pakistan partition.
